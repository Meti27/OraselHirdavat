Welding Equipment

HOMEPAGE / PRODUCTS / OCCUPATIONAL / SAFETY / PRODUCTS

Welding equipment is the products used when working with welding machines. These machines mostly work for joining metal and thermoplastic materials.

Since it is used for manufacturing purposes, the joining process takes place by using heat and pressure separately or together. Metal welding is the process that is done by adding materials that are similar in kind and melting ranges to each other.

The technique of combining metal materials with the added filling material as a result of melting is performed. Welding is a complementary action applied at the end of the process. It causes different materials to be divided into varieties in accordance with different melting and resistance standards and unique technical use in this field.

Welding is an important application made by combining materials of different structures in many areas such as open and closed areas, underwater and outer space. At the same time, its usage areas have led to the development of techniques and various methods. Regardless of the technique of welding, it should be done by experts in the field by wearing protective clothing. In addition to dangerous risks such as high heat and electricity that occur during welding, ultraviolet rays and toxic fumes occur.

If the wrong filler material, wrong method and technique is applied during welding, the process will be unsuccessful. This situation eventually results in health problems. Today, the most widely used welding types are energy beam sources, MMA arc welding, solid state welding methods, gas metal arc welding, TIG welding, cored wire arc welding, MMA arc welding, electrical resistance welding, oxy-acetylene welding.

Covered electrode arc welding, which is one of the welding techniques that can be done by hand, is performed in open and closed areas. It is combined with the heat required for welding and the process is carried out thanks to the arc formed at the junction of the materials. Electrodes are used to create this arc. It is the application used in the joining of metals.

It is a method used because the welding equipment is portable and light when there is narrow and movement difficulties. Electric resistance welding is a low-pollution welding technique. It is a technique applied to metal with high current through metals. Arc welding with cored wire is an application made by the use of the cored wire electrode and the arc formed by the combination of metal parts. Oxy-acetylene sources are very easy to use and their equipment is affordable. It is the most used resource application. Welding flame occurs up to 3100 degrees.

It is formed by the combustion of acetylene and oxygen together. This application is also a technique used in cutting metals. It is heavily preferred in channels and pipes. It is a technique applied thanks to the heat of welding, which is formed by the combustion of the electrode used in gas metal arc welding. The electrode forms the metal required for welding by melting. TIG welding, on the other hand, is provided by the tungsten electrode, thanks to the electrode that does not consume the heat required for welding. The most used welding equipments today are; bag welding machine, pipe welding machine, spot welding machine, inverter welding machine, transformer welding machine and rectifier welding machine. Inverter welding machines are the most preferred in industry and manufacturing. It is preferred because it works with high energy and consumes less electricity. The purpose of use of all welding machines and equipment is different.
