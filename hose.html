Industrial Hoses

Industrial hoses are special hoses that can show resistance at high pressures and temperatures, and can be used in industrial areas in caustic, corrosive and all weather conditions. Industrial hoses are manufactured with a special rubber outer sheath that is resistant to chemical heat and lubricant, reinforced with a metal braid or textile sheath. The connection of the hoses with the unions are those that carry high pressures and are heavy-duty. Industrial hoses are generally used in hydraulic, pneumatic and chemical processing systems.

Hydraulic Hoses

They are hydraulic circuit elements that are more preferred in industrial environments and provide the transmission of fluid pressure to the hydraulic system. The features of synthetic rubber, synthetic textile and steel wire braid layers that increase durability are also important details in the systems where hydraulic hoses will be used. Hydraulic hoses are an indispensable element of a hydraulic circuit in order to ensure the continuity of operation and continuity in industrial environments.

Fiberglass and Silicone Coated Hoses

In order to increase the durability of the hydraulic hoses used and to strengthen the technical features, as Votek Industry, we offer our products to our customers, where silicone coating is applied to the hoses together with glass fiber.

Stainless Steel Flex Braided Hoses

In order to best meet the needs of our customers according to the areas they work in, our stainless steel flex braided hoses, which we produce with our expert and technical team, are resistant to corrosion, abrasion and very high temperatures.

Composite Hoses

Composite hoses are covered with a sheath made of polypropylene (PP) material to increase friction and impact resistance. Its inner and outer surfaces are reinforced with steel spiral wire. It is used in fuel stations, ship refueling, chemical, food, steam absorption and discharge places.

Clamps

Votek Industry, which offers a wide range of products in the fastener sector, continues to expand its product range by incorporating hose clamps group products.


Today, clamp types that offer practical solutions promise a wide range of use and durability.


Hose Clamps group products can be used in different areas such as heating-cooling systems, exhaust systems, passenger safety systems, steering systems, constant velocity joints, medical applications, white goods applications, food and beverage systems.


Our range of clamps provide uniform compression and surface pressure at 360 degrees. With Toothlook technology, it provides very high and permanent compression ratios and high resistance to pressure and expansion. The increased ear width allows the diameter range to be expanded.


We use clamp products of high quality standards to ensure that every connection works properly in the long run. In this way, we always try to provide a secure and correct connection. Burr-free edges have a significant advantage over conventional edges in order to protect the clamped parts from damage and prevent possible accidents during assembly.


The safety hook on the clamps prevents unwanted opening. In this special design, steps or overlaps on the inner circumference of the clamp are eliminated.


Because the tongue fits in the groove, the “tongue and groove” feature exerts 360-degree pressure on the assembled parts. This ensures that a full radial force is continuously applied around the coupling diameter of the clamp. The new CrossTech feature offers an ultra-low profile design to save space and reduce instability in rotating parts. The unique "ToothLock®" thread locking feature offers extremely high and stable compression ratios, exceptional expansion resistance and is strong enough for the toughest connections.


Our clamp group products include Ear Hose Clamps, Low Profile Clamps, Screw and Universal Clamps, Collapsible Ring Clamps, Hanger Clamps and Profile Clamps.


You can contact the experienced sales representatives of Votek Industry to get more information about Hose Clamps group products and to find the most suitable clamp model for your business.
